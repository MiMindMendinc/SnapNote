# SnapNote Use Cases

Real-world scenarios where SnapNote Next Gen helps families, students, and caregivers — all processing stays in the browser.

---

## School & students

### Homework board → clean notes
**Scenario:** A student photographs the classroom whiteboard before leaving.  
**SnapNote flow:** Upload → OCR → Summary → Export `.txt` → paste into notes app.  
**Why it helps:** Faster than retyping; summary highlights the main assignment.

### Handout key points
**Scenario:** A long printed handout needs a quick study sheet.  
**SnapNote flow:** Photo → OCR → Keywords → Ask "what is due?" or "when is the test?"  
**Why it helps:** Pulls dates and action items without reading every line.

### Bilingual family support (Pro Mode)
**Scenario:** A caregiver reads English school notices but shares updates in Spanish.  
**SnapNote flow:** Enable Pro Mode → get headline + Spanish summary → copy to family chat.  
**Why it helps:** Offline dictionary translation for quick sharing (not certified translation).

## Family & home

### Receipt tracking
**Scenario:** Track a store receipt for returns or budgeting.  
**SnapNote flow:** Photo → OCR → Keywords (store, total, date) → Export.  
**Why it helps:** Searchable text from crumpled receipts.

### Appointment cards
**Scenario:** Doctor or dentist appointment card with small print.  
**SnapNote flow:** Photo → OCR → Ask "when is the appointment?"  
**Why it helps:** Large readable text + quick answer from the note.

### Recipe clipping
**Scenario:** Grandma's handwritten recipe card.  
**SnapNote flow:** Photo → OCR → Copy text → save in notes.  
**Why it helps:** Preserves family recipes as digital text.

---

## Caregivers & community

### Medication instructions
**Scenario:** Pharmacy label or discharge paperwork (non-emergency reference).  
**SnapNote flow:** Photo → OCR → Summary → Ask about dosage timing.  
**Important:** SnapNote is **not** medical advice. Always confirm with a pharmacist or clinician.

### Community flyer
**Scenario:** Flyer for a food pantry or community event.  
**SnapNote flow:** Photo → OCR → Headline (Pro Mode) → share summary.  
**Why it helps:** Quick digest for posting in a group chat.

### Form pre-fill prep
**Scenario:** Paper intake form before an office visit.  
**SnapNote flow:** Photo → OCR → Copy sections → paste into digital forms.  
**Why it helps:** Reduces manual retyping.
