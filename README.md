# SnapNote ✨

**Turn any photo into clean, intelligent notes — 100% in your browser. Zero data leaves your device.**

[Live Demo](https://mimindmendinc.github.io/snapnote) • [Install as App] • [GitHub](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/snapnote)

![SnapNote in action — clean, calm, offline-first interface](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x600/0f766e/ffffff?text=SnapNote+Demo+GIF+Coming+Soon)

## Why This Exists

Families seeking mental health support are already carrying enough weight.  
The last thing they need is slow servers, privacy worries, or clunky tools when they're trying to organize doctor's notes, school forms, therapy worksheets, or personal journals.

**SnapNote removes every single barrier.**

Snap a photo → get perfect text, smart summaries, keywords, and instant answers — all running locally, instantly, offline.

## ✨ What You Can Do in Seconds

- Drag & drop or take a photo of any handwritten or printed page  
- Watch accurate OCR happen right in your browser  
- Ask natural questions about the content and get real answers (on-device BERT)  
- Get extractive summaries + top keywords automatically  
- Flip on 🤯 Pro Mode for auto-headlines and simple on-device translation  
- Install it as a real app on your phone or desktop — works forever with no internet  

Everything is private by design. No accounts. No tracking. No cloud.
